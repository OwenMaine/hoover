From a Simple Idea to a Chef's Trusted Partner.

This is our story.

Our Journey

A timeline of passion and precision.

2013

The Idea

Our founder, a veteran of the food service industry, saw a gap: restaurants needed a supplier that operated with the same precision and passion as their own kitchens. Hoover Restaurants, LLC was born from this simple idea.

2016

First Warehouse

After three years of building a reputation for reliability out of a small facility, we opened our first dedicated warehouse in Suwanee, GA. This allowed us to expand our product lines and implement a state-of-the-art inventory system.

2020

Expanding Our Family

We launched our first client portal and expanded our delivery fleet, focusing on technology to improve the customer experience. Our client base grew to over 50 restaurant groups across Georgia, cementing our status as a key partner.

Today

Focused on the Future

With an unwavering commitment to quality and service, we continue to innovate, source unique products, and build lasting relationships. Your success is our mission.

"We don't just sell ingredients; we provide the foundation for culinary creation. Every box we deliver carries our promise of quality, reliability, and respect for the craft. We're here to be the partner I wish I had when I was in the kitchen."

- James R. Hoover

Founder & CEO, Hoover Restaurants, LLC
